Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence relates to a variety of different skills and abilities which one can learn throughout their life. The ability to manage emotions, social skills and self-awarness as well as many more. It can be seen as a scale for your ability to understand and work in situations which revolve outside of just academic skills.

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ focuses on emotional intelligence and the ability to understand yourself as well as those around you. IQ focuses on your specific academic intelligence as well as your ability to problem solve in a task based assessment.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is important because it judges your skills when working in social situations. Especially in the coding space, being able to not only work with others but be able to manage your emotions and time management when things get tough is super important. If you are able to work well with others as well as be concious of your own abilities or places you need to grow, it becomes super vital when trying to find work in this area.
